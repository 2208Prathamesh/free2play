Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

At Free2Play, accessible from free2play.in, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Free2Play and how we use it.

Log Files

Free2Play follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, Free2Play uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited.

Third Party Privacy Policies

Free2Play's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information.
